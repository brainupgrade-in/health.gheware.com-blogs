Frequently Asked Questions

Are these recipes suitable for all diabetics?

These recipes are designed to be lower glycemic and diabetes-friendly, but individual responses vary. Always monitor your blood sugar and consult your healthcare provider before making significant dietary changes.

Do I need special ingredients?

Most ingredients are commonly available in Indian grocery stores. We've included a complete shopping list. Items like ragi flour, millets, and besan are increasingly available in regular supermarkets too.

How long do the recipes take to prepare?

Quick options take 5-10 minutes. Regular recipes take 15-20 minutes. Some items like idli/dosa batter need overnight fermentation, but actual cooking is quick.

Can I meal prep these recipes?

Absolutely! The guide includes detailed meal prep instructions, including what can be made ahead, freezer-friendly items, and a Sunday prep routine for the week.

Are these recipes vegetarian?

Most recipes are vegetarian. We've included 1-2 egg-based options for those who eat eggs. All can be adapted to your dietary preferences.

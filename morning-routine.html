‚ùì Frequently Asked Questions

Is this checklist suitable for Type 1 and Type 2 diabetics?

This guide is primarily designed for Type 2 diabetics and pre-diabetics. Type 1 diabetics should consult their endocrinologist before making any changes to their routine, especially regarding medication and exercise timing.

How quickly will I see results?

Most people notice improvements in their fasting blood sugar within 1-2 weeks of consistently following the routine. For significant HbA1c improvements, give it 2-3 months of consistent practice.

Can I modify the routine to fit my schedule?

Absolutely! The guide provides optimal timings, but consistency matters more than perfection. Adjust the times to fit your lifestyle while maintaining the sequence and spacing between activities.

Is the methi water safe for everyone?

Methi water is generally safe for most people. However, pregnant women, people on blood thinners, or those with specific allergies should consult their doctor first. Start with small amounts to check tolerance.

What Traditional CGM Apps Do Well

Before comparing, let's acknowledge where Dexcom G7, Freestyle Libre 3, and their companion apps excel:

1. Real-Time Glucose Monitoring (CGM Manufacturer Apps)

What they offer:

  • Continuous glucose readings every 1-5 minutes (288 data points/day vs 4-6 finger pricks)
  • Live glucose trend arrows showing direction (‚Üë rising fast, ‚Üí steady, ‚Üì dropping)
  • Current glucose value displayed on phone, watch, or dedicated receiver
  • Graph view showing last 1-24 hours of glucose history

Why this matters: Real-time monitoring is critical for immediate safety. If your glucose drops to 65 mg/dL at 3 AM, you need an alarm NOW‚Äînot a weekly insight report.

2. Customizable Alarms and Alerts

What they offer:

  • Low glucose alarms (e.g., alert when below 70 mg/dL)
  • High glucose alarms (e.g., alert when above 180 mg/dL)
  • Urgent low alarms (e.g., below 55 mg/dL‚Äîcannot be disabled)
  • Rate-of-change alerts (e.g., dropping >2 mg/dL per minute)
  • Lost signal alerts when sensor disconnects

Why this matters: Hypoglycemia (low blood sugar) can be life-threatening. Real-time alarms prevent medical emergencies.

3. Sensor Management Features

What they offer:

  • Sensor pairing via Bluetooth/NFC to phone
  • Sensor expiration tracking (10-14 day sensor life)
  • Calibration (for some CGM models requiring finger-prick verification)
  • Troubleshooting for sensor errors, connection issues

Why this matters: CGM hardware requires active management. Only manufacturer apps can handle sensor-specific functions.

4. Detailed Glucose-Only Reports (Companion Apps: Clarity, LibreView)

What they offer:

  • Ambulatory Glucose Profile (AGP)‚Äîthe gold standard glucose report showing median glucose, variability, and time ranges
  • Time in Range (TIR) percentages broken down by day, week, month
  • Glucose patterns (morning highs, post-meal spikes, overnight lows)
  • Daily overlay charts showing glucose patterns by time of day
  • Shareable PDF reports for healthcare providers

Why this matters: Endocrinologists rely on AGP reports to adjust medication. These companion apps generate clinic-quality reports for free.

Bottom Line: Traditional CGM apps are essential for diabetes management. They handle real-time safety, sensor operations, and comprehensive glucose reporting. Health Gheware doesn't replace these‚Äîit adds a layer they don't provide.

The 5 Critical Gaps in CGM Apps

Here's where traditional CGM apps fall short‚Äîand where most users get stuck:

Gap #1: No Multi-Data Correlation

The Problem: CGM apps only see glucose. They completely miss sleep quality, exercise timing, meal composition, stress levels, and medications‚Äîall of which directly impact your blood sugar.

Reality: Your glucose is affected by multiple factors simultaneously:

  • Evening strength training causes delayed lows 8-12 hours later

  • Overnight low at 2 AM

Before You Begin

How to Use This Book

📖

Read Sequentially First

Parts I through III build upon each other. Start with understanding diabetes, then move to eating strategies, and finally tracking & management.

🔖

Reference Anytime

The GI guide, carb counting tables, and medication reference are designed to be used daily. Bookmark these chapters for quick access.

👨‍👩‍👧‍👦

Share with Family

Diabetes management is a family effort. Share the recipes and meal plans with whoever cooks at home for maximum impact.

📝

Use the Trackers

The 30-day journals and trackers (Chapters 11-12) are the most powerful parts of this book. Print or photocopy them and use them daily.

⚠️

Medical Disclaimer

This book is educational. Always consult your doctor before changing medications, diet, or exercise routines. Your doctor knows your body best.

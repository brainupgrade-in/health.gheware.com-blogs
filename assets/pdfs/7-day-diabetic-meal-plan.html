🍛 7-Day Indian Diabetic Meal Plan

Balanced, delicious meals designed for blood sugar control
by Health Gheware

⚠️ Disclaimer: This is a general guide (~1500-1600 kcal/day). Consult your doctor or dietician for a plan tailored to your needs, medications, and health goals.

📅 Monday

Breakfast (8 AM)
2 Idli + Sambar (1 cup) + Coconut Chutney (1 tbsp) ~280 kcal · 42g carbs
Mid-Morning Snack (10:30 AM)
1 small Guava + 10 Almonds ~120 kcal · 12g carbs
Lunch (1 PM)
2 Multigrain Roti + Palak Dal (1 cup) + Cucumber Raita (½ cup) + Salad ~420 kcal · 48g carbs
Evening Snack (4:30 PM)
Masala Buttermilk (1 glass) + 2 Khakhra ~130 kcal · 18g carbs
Dinner (7:30 PM)
1 Roti + Lauki Sabzi (1 cup) + Moong Dal (½ cup) ~350 kcal · 38g carbs
Total: ~1300 kcal · ~158g carbs

📅 Tuesday

Breakfast (8 AM)
Vegetable Poha (1.5 cups) with peanuts + Lemon ~290 kcal · 40g carbs
Mid-Morning Snack (10:30 AM)
1 small Apple + 5 Walnuts ~140 kcal · 15g carbs
Lunch (1 PM)
Brown Rice (¾ cup) + Rajma Curry (1 cup) + Salad + Curd (½ cup) ~450 kcal · 55g carbs
Evening Snack (4:30 PM)
Roasted Chana (¼ cup) + Green Tea ~100 kcal · 14g carbs
Dinner (7:30 PM)
2 Bajra Roti + Bhindi Sabzi (1 cup) + Dal Tadka (½ cup) ~380 kcal · 42g carbs
Total: ~1360 kcal · ~166g carbs

📅 Wednesday

Breakfast (8 AM)
Moong Dal Chilla (2) + Mint Chutney + 1 cup Chai (no sugar) ~260 kcal · 30g carbs
Mid-Morning Snack (10:30 AM)
1 small Orange + Handful of Peanuts ~130 kcal · 14g carbs
Lunch (1 PM)
2 Roti + Chole (1 cup, less oil) + Onion-Tomato Salad + Buttermilk ~440 kcal · 52g carbs
Evening Snack (4:30 PM)
Sprouts Chaat (½ cup) with lemon & chaat masala ~110 kcal · 15g carbs
Dinner (7:30 PM)
1 Roti + Tinda Sabzi (1 cup) + Masoor Dal (½ cup) ~340 kcal · 40g carbs
Total: ~1280 kcal · ~151g carbs

📅 Thursday

Breakfast (8 AM)
Vegetable Upma (1.5 cups) + Coconut Chutney ~280 kcal · 38g carbs
Mid-Morning Snack (10:30 AM)
1 small Pear + 8 Cashews ~130 kcal · 16g carbs
Lunch (1 PM)
Curd Rice (1 cup) + Sambar (1 cup) + Papad + Carrot Salad ~400 kcal · 50g carbs
Evening Snack (4:30 PM)
1 Multigrain Toast + Sugar-free Peanut Butter (1 tbsp) ~140 kcal · 14g carbs
Dinner (7:30 PM)
2 Jowar Roti + Baingan Bharta (1 cup) + Yellow Dal (½ cup) ~370 kcal · 44g carbs
Total: ~1320 kcal · ~162g carbs

📅 Friday

Breakfast (8 AM)
Besan Chilla (2) + Tomato Chutney + Chai (no sugar) ~270 kcal · 28g carbs
Mid-Morning Snack (10:30 AM)
Cucumber sticks + Hummus (3 tbsp) ~110 kcal · 10g carbs
Lunch (1 PM)
2 Roti + Mixed Veg Curry (1 cup) + Dal Fry (½ cup) + Salad ~430 kcal · 50g carbs
Evening Snack (4:30 PM)
Makhana (Foxnuts) roasted (¼ cup) + Green Tea ~90 kcal · 12g carbs
Dinner (7:30 PM)
1 Roti + Karela Sabzi (1 cup) + Moong Dal (½ cup) ~330 kcal · 36g carbs
Total: ~1230 kcal · ~136g carbs

📅 Saturday

Breakfast (8 AM)
1 Masala Dosa (small) + Sambar (½ cup) + Chutney ~310 kcal · 44g carbs
Mid-Morning Snack (10:30 AM)
1 small Banana (semi-ripe) + 6 Almonds ~140 kcal · 18g carbs
Lunch (1 PM)
Jeera Rice (¾ cup) + Kadhi Pakora (1 cup) + Salad ~430 kcal · 52g carbs
Evening Snack (4:30 PM)
Dhokla (2 pieces) + Green Chutney ~120 kcal · 16g carbs
Dinner (7:30 PM)
2 Roti + Methi Aloo (light oil, 1 cup) + Arhar Dal (½ cup) ~400 kcal · 48g carbs
Total: ~1400 kcal · ~178g carbs

📅 Sunday

Breakfast (8 AM)
Stuffed Paratha (1 Aloo/Paneer, light oil) + Curd (½ cup) + Pickle ~320 kcal · 38g carbs
Mid-Morning Snack (10:30 AM)
Papaya (1 cup) + 5 Pistachios ~100 kcal · 14g carbs
Lunch (1 PM)
Brown Rice Pulao (¾ cup) + Paneer Bhurji (½ cup) + Raita + Salad ~460 kcal · 48g carbs
Evening Snack (4:30 PM)
Murmura (Puffed Rice) Bhel (1 cup) ~110 kcal · 20g carbs
Dinner (7:30 PM)
2 Roti + Torai/Ridge Gourd Sabzi (1 cup) + Chana Dal (½ cup) ~370 kcal · 44g carbs
Total: ~1360 kcal · ~164g carbs

🛒 Weekly Shopping List

  • Multigrain / Bajra / Jowar Atta — 1 kg
  • Brown Rice — 500g
  • Moong Dal, Masoor Dal, Arhar Dal, Chana Dal — 250g each
  • Rajma, Chole — 250g each
  • Poha, Rava (for upma) — 250g each
  • Besan — 250g
  • Idli/Dosa batter — 500g (or make fresh)
  • Fresh Vegetables: Palak, Lauki, Bhindi, Baingan, Karela, Tinda, Torai, Methi, Mixed Veg
  • Tomatoes, Onions, Cucumber, Carrots — as needed
  • Fruits: Guava, Apple, Orange, Pear, Papaya (1 each)
  • 1 semi-ripe Banana
  • Curd / Buttermilk — 1 L
  • Paneer — 200g
  • Almonds, Walnuts, Cashews, Peanuts, Pistachios — small packets
  • Roasted Chana, Makhana, Murmura — 100g each
  • Khakhra — 1 pack
  • Sprouts — 200g
  • Coconut (for chutney), Lemons, Green Chillies, Ginger, Curry Leaves
  • Spices: Jeera, Haldi, Coriander, Red Chilli, Garam Masala, Chaat Masala
  • Cooking Oil (Mustard/Olive) — 500ml
  • Green Tea — 1 box

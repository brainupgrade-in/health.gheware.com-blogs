## Introduction

Managing diabetes doesn't mean giving up on delicious Indian breakfasts. This recipe collection reimagines traditional favorites with diabetes-friendly modifications that maintain authentic flavors while keeping blood sugar stable.

### Key Principles for Diabetes-Friendly Indian Breakfast:

- **Increase fiber content** using whole grains, millets, and vegetables
- **Reduce refined carbs** by substituting with healthier alternatives
- **Add protein** to slow down glucose absorption
- **Include healthy fats** for satiety and nutrient absorption
- **Control portions** while maximizing nutrition

## Understanding Glycemic Index

| GI Rating | Range | Effect on Blood Sugar |
|-----------|-------|----------------------|
| **Low** | 0-55 | Slow, gradual rise |
| **Medium** | 56-69 | Moderate rise |
| **High** | 70+ | Rapid spike (avoid) |
